# Privacy Policy — Tank Battle: Boss Arena

_Last updated: 2026-06-18_

This Privacy Policy explains how the game **Tank Battle: Boss Arena** ("the App", "we") handles information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

## Information we collect
We (the developer) do **not** collect, store, or share any personal information that identifies you. The App does not require an account, login, or registration.

The App stores your level progress (the highest unlocked level) **locally on your device only**. This never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the App.

## Advertising (Google AdMob)
The App displays ads through **Google AdMob** (app-open, rewarded video, and banner ads). To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may collect and process:
- Advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
- Device information (device model, OS version, language, coarse region)
- App interaction and ad-interaction data

This processing is performed by Google, not by us. Google's use of data is governed by:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from sites/apps that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can reset or limit ad personalization in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

## Children
The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Ads are requested with standard settings; if you target a younger audience, configure AdMob/Play accordingly.

## Permissions
The App requests Internet access (used by the ad SDK). It does not access your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or precise location.

## Data retention & deletion
We do not hold any personal data, so there is nothing for us to retain or delete. Local progress is deleted by uninstalling the App. For ad-related data, refer to Google's policies above.

## Changes
We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
