# Privacy Policy — AnimalTower

_Last updated: 2026-06-23_

This privacy policy explains how the mobile game **AnimalTower** ("the App", "we") handles information.

## Information we collect
The App itself does **not** ask for, collect, or store any personally identifiable information (no account, no login, no name, email, or location entry).

The App uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob, provided by Google, may automatically collect and process certain data, including:
- Device advertising identifier (Advertising ID / App Set ID)
- Coarse device and app information (device model, OS version, app version)
- General, non-precise usage and ad-interaction data

This data is used to serve and measure advertisements and to prevent fraud.

## How data is used and shared
Ad-related data is collected and processed by Google as our advertising partner. We do not sell your data. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:
- https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

## Data security
All ad and analytics data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

## Children
The App is **not directed to children under 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Advertising is configured for a general (13+) audience.

## Your choices
You can reset or limit ad personalization via your device settings:
- Android: Settings › Google › Ads › Reset/Delete advertising ID, or "Opt out of Ads Personalization".
